California & Lake Tahoe Area Map Collection

All you need for a visit to the region, the California & Lake Tahoe Area Map Collection offers four comprehensive maps to guide you to the grand jewels of this great territory.

 "To breathe the same air as the angels, you must go to Tahoe" -Mark Twain Emerald Bay

A beautiful collection for the use of hikers and other self-propelled travelers of Greater Lake Tahoe & Sierra Nevada Wilderness. Printed on waterproof and tear resistant material.

Maps Contains areas such as Loon Lakem Echo Lake, Lake Aloha, Emerald Bay, Wrights Lake, Fallen Leaf, Rubicon River, D.L. Bliss State Park, P.C.T., Rockbound Valley, Tahoe Rim Trail and more.

These maps will get you there.

  • California State Road Map
  • Lake Tahoe Basin 
  • Lake Tahoe/Carson City Map
  • Lake Tahoe & Tahoe Rim Trail Maps-Tom Harrison
  • Sierra Nevadas Desolation Wilderness- Tom Harrison
  • Truckee River Fishing Access Map
  • Nevada State Map- LARGE Print
